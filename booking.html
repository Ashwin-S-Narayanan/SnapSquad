Traditional Photography

Traditional photography captures the essence of an event or a moment through posed photographs and formal shots. This style focuses on classic poses, carefully arranged scenes, and well-lit compositions. It emphasizes the importance of capturing every detail, from the bride's dress to the groom's expression.

Traditional Videography

Traditional videography involves capturing events with a structured and formal approach. This style includes staged scenes, interviews, and detailed coverage of the event. It emphasizes continuity and narrative flow, ensuring all critical moments are recorded. Videographers use professional equipment to ensure high-quality footage.

Candid Photography

Candid photography captures spontaneous moments without posing or interfering with the subjects. This style focuses on natural expressions, genuine emotions, and unscripted interactions. Photographers blend into the background, allowing events to unfold naturally.

Candid Videography

Candid videography involves capturing real-life moments and interactions as they happen. This style is less intrusive, focusing on natural expressions and genuine emotions. Videographers use minimal staging, allowing events to flow naturally. It's perfect for capturing the true atmosphere of an event, showcasing unscripted reactions and interactions.

Drone

Drone photography provides a unique aerial perspective, capturing stunning views from above. This style uses drones equipped with cameras to photograph landscapes, events, and structures. It's perfect for capturing expansive views, architectural details, and large gatherings. Drone photography offers angles and compositions that are impossible from the ground.
